## Method 2: Traditional Method

### When to Use
- Only adding a house (no rooms yet)
- Adding rooms later
- Prefer step-by-step approach

### Steps

1. **Add House**
   - Go to `/admin/houses`
   - Click **"Add House Only"**
   - Fill in house details
   - Click "Create"

2. **Add Rooms (Later)**
   - Click "Rooms" link on the house
   - Click "Add Room"
   - Fill in room details
   - Click "Create"
   - Repeat for each room

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Quick Setup

1. **Pre-plan your rooms**
   - Know how many rooms you need
   - Have naming scheme ready (e.g., "Room 101", "Master Bedroom")

2. **Use bulk add buttons**
   - Click "+ Add 3 Rooms" or "+ Add 5 Rooms" 
   - Auto-fills with "Room 1", "Room 2", etc.
   - Rename as needed

3. **Review carefully**
   - Check all room names are correct
   - Verify capacities (1 or 2 people)
   - Use Step 3 review to catch mistakes

4. **Common patterns**
   - **4-bedroom house**: Use "+ Add 5 Rooms" â†’ Remove 1
   - **6-bedroom house**: Use "+ Add 5 Rooms" â†’ Add 1 more
   - **3-bedroom house**: Use "+ Add 3 Rooms"

### For Traditional Method

1. **When adding rooms one-by-one**
   - Use consistent naming (Room 1, Room 2, etc.)
   - Set capacity correctly (can't easily change later)
   - Archive instead of delete if mistakes

2. **Edit house details first**
   - Add complete address
   - Verify name spelling
   - Then add rooms

## Troubleshooting

### Quick Setup Issues

**Q: I made a mistake on Step 1, can I go back?**
A: Yes! Click the "â† Back" button on Step 2 or 3

**Q: Can I edit rooms after creation?**
A: Yes, go to the house's rooms page and click "Edit" on any room

**Q: What if I add too many rooms?**
A: Use the X button to remove rooms before final creation, or archive them after

**Q: Do I have to fill in all room labels?**
A: No, empty rooms will be skipped. But at least one room is required.

### Traditional Method Issues

**Q: I forgot to add rooms, how do I add them now?**
A: Click the "Rooms" link on your house in the houses list

**Q: Can I bulk add rooms later?**
A: Not with traditional method. Use the rooms page to add one at a time, or consider deleting the house and using Quick Setup instead.
